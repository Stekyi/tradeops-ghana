## #1 — Shea butter | EXPORT (sell abroad)
**Opportunity Score: 🟢 84/100 | Margin: 🟢 45%**

- **Ghana exports:** $174.0M/year, growing **+116.0% YoY**
- **Best market:** Netherlands (demand growing +25%/yr)
- **Ghana producer price:** $750/MT
- **Starting order:** GHC 60,000 buys ~**5.2 MT**
- **Price premium:** HIGH

**Target markets:**
  - Netherlands: +25% demand growth, high premium
  - France: +20% demand growth, high premium
  - South Korea: +30% demand growth, high premium

**Next steps (Weiss method):**
  - [ ] Register with GEPA Ghana as an exporter (free SME program)
  - [ ] Contact 3 international buyers via trade leads (intracen.org/trade-leads)
  - [ ] Get a product sample tested and certified (Ghana Standards Authority)
  - [ ] Confirm packaging/labelling requirements for target market
  - [ ] Start with small air-freight sample before full sea container

## #2 — Rice | IMPORT (distribute in Ghana)
**Opportunity Score: 🟢 70/100 | Margin: 🟡 22%**

- **Ghana imports:** $487.0M/year, growing **+23.0% YoY**
- **Cheapest source:** India at **$290/MT**
- **Starting order:** GHC 60,000 buys ~**13.4 MT**

**Supply sources:**
  - India: $290/MT (medium distance)
  - Vietnam: $310/MT (far distance)
  - Thailand: $350/MT (far distance)

**Next steps (Weiss method):**
  - [ ] Contact GEPA Ghana (gepaghana.org) for approved import supplier lists
  - [ ] Visit 3 wholesalers in Accra/Kumasi to confirm retail price and demand
  - [ ] Request sample order (≤ GHC 5,000) before full commitment
  - [ ] Check Ghana Customs (customs.gov.gh) for applicable duty rates
  - [ ] Secure a buyer (retailer/wholesaler) with written purchase commitment first
